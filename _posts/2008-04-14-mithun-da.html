Its high time that I stop thinking and start blogging about Mithun, unarguably Mithun da is the greatest icon in hindi movies. Once he said at the Filmfare awards that 'Amitabh Bacchan is a star till Dharavi, its my territory beyond Dharavi .. '  man you need balls of steel to say that.

I spend most of my productive hours hunting for Mithun movies like Cheetah , Loha, Suraj, Shapath, Heeralal Pannalal, Loha, Jallad, Chandal ... list is endless, I tasted success when I saw 'Gunda', I swear it was one memorable experience in my life !  Over the years I have been collecting famous dialogues of Mithun here are the most mesmerizing ones, feel free to add yours !
